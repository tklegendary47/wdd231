About the Harare Chamber

Championing business growth since 1974

Our Mission

To connect, elevate, and champion businesses across Harare through advocacy, world‑class networking, and relentless innovation. We believe a rising tide lifts all boats – every entrepreneur, from the market stall to the boardroom, deserves a voice and a platform.
