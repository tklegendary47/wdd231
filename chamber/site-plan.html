TalentTrack

Smart Job Board for Modern Job Seekers

Site Name

TalentTrack – This name reflects a platform that helps users track and manage job opportunities efficiently while maintaining a modern and user-friendly experience.

Site Purpose

TalentTrack provides a clean and intuitive platform where users can browse job listings, filter opportunities based on preferences, and save jobs they are interested in. The site focuses on simplifying the job search process while making it more organized and efficient.

Scenarios

  • What jobs match my skills and preferred location?
  • How can I save or track jobs I want to apply for later?

Color Scheme

  • #2563EB (Blue) – Headings, header, buttons
  • #F1F5F9 (Light Gray) – Background
  • #10B981 (Green) – Accents and CTAs

Typography

  • Inter – Body text
